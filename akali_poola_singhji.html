Akali Poola Singh

Akali Phoola Singh was a legendary Sikh warrior and commander of the Sikh Khalsa Army during Maharaja Ranjit Singh's rule. He was known for his bravery, discipline, and commitment to Sikh principles. He played a crucial role in expanding the Sikh Empire and leading successful military campaigns against Afghan invaders. Akali Phoola Singh was a fearless general who upheld justice and Sikh sovereignty. His leadership in the Battle of Nowshera in 1823, where he ultimately sacrificed his life, remains an inspiring tale of valor in Sikh history.
