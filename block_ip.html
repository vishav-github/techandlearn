Block IP

When an account fails to log in multiple times, the IP address will be blocked for security reasons.

  • Unblock IP: Go to Settings > Blocked IPs and delete the blocked address.
  • Whitelist IP: Navigate to Settings > Whitelisted IPs and click “Add” to allow access.
  • Increase login attempts: Adjust settings in Operation Settings to a maximum of 10 failed logins.
  • Blocked users can contact support via email at BlockedIPMail.

Additional Information

  • You can add this blocked IP address to the whitelist by going to Settings > Whitelisted IPs (View Screenshot) and clicking “Add.”
  • Increase the number of allowed failed log-ins by navigating to Settings > Operation Settings (View Screenshot). The maximum allowed attempts are 10.
  • Add an email contact at Settings > Operation Settings > BlockedIPMail (View Screenshot) so blocked users can request unblocking.
